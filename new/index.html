About ACP

Antarcticaa College of Pharmacy was established in the year 1987. This is situated at about 4 kms from Tirunelveli New Bus Stand. The Campus spread over 14.96 acres of Land economically utilized for the college play fields and Garden.

It is approved by Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi, and Directorate of Medical Education, Chennai, TamilNadu.
